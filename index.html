About

Craftsmen Interiors, Inc. established in 1982 is a flooring contractor that furnishes and installs all types of flooring (i.e. hardwood, carpet, vinyl, ceramic, marble, stone, etc.). Our primary focus is commercial work, but we also do residential applications. Two company officers have a combined experience of 75 years in the business. We believe in the highest quality of work and that is what distinguishes Craftsmen Interiors, Inc. from other flooring contractors.

Meet the Team

Union Affiliation

Craftsmen Interiors, Inc. employs members of Local 1310 Floor Layers Union. All apprentices and journeymen undergo training in the International Standards and Training Alliance (INSTALL), a four-year certification process. We are affiliated with the Tile Setters Local 18 of Missouri who also undergoes an extensive apprenticeship program to make them professional installers.
