Don Nosal, President

Don has over 37 years of experience in the flooring business. After graduating from the University of Missouri-Columbia in 1983, he started working at Craftsmen Interiors Inc. While at Craftsmen Interiors, he has been actively involved in the Local Ceramic Tile Apprenticeship board as well as the Flooring Industry Council Public Relations Board and is on the Board of Directors for the Flooring Industry Council of Greater St. Louis.

Jeff Beckmann, Vice President

Jeff has 37 years of experience in the flooring industry and has been a member in good standing of the local Floor Layers Union 1310 for the past 37 years. Jeff has participated in the UBC Journeymen Building Leadership Program in Las Vegas and continues to stay updated on the newest material and installation techniques.
